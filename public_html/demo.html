The system offers three rider types including:
Customer: pay as-you-go customer
Daily: 24-hour pass customer
Subscriber: deluxe and standard monthly member customer
The "unknown" indicates the ride of which the rider type is missing. Each rider type has its own pricing scheme.

The chart "Average Daily Ride Counts versus Weather" measures the average daily ride counts per station in the different weather conditions including:
Good: sunny and clear
Mild: mist or blowing
Poor: rain, fog or freezing
Severe: snow or thunderstorm

The chart "Average Daily Ride Counts versus Temperature" measures the average daily ride counts per station in the different temperature conditions, and the temperature unit is centigrade.

The plot "Temporal Ride Patterns in the course of day" compares the average hourly ride counts per station in the course of day which is on the weekend or not.
